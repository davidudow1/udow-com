About

Operator. Builder. Acquirer.

I started early. In 1988, while studying at the University of Manitoba, I launched Clearview Window Cleaners in Winnipeg — a summer job that turned into a year-round operation. When I moved to Vancouver in 1994, I handed it to a trusted partner. It's still running today, 38 years later. In the mid-90s I co-founded NetSet Home Solutions, helping Vancouver businesses navigate the early internet — field technicians, small business consulting, web strategy. We built a real team before winding down as the dot-com bubble burst. By then I was already focused on what came next.

In 2000, I co-founded Ziptrek Ecotours in Whistler with nothing but an idea — no infrastructure, no team, no playbook. We built it into one of the most recognized adventure tourism brands in North America, with operations spanning Whistler and Queenstown, New Zealand. Over 150 employees. Profitable. Running without founder dependency. I transitioned to the Board in 2012 and remain an active Director and shareholder today.

In 2014, I acquired Pink Solution, an eco-friendly consumer cleaning brand sold nationally across Canada. I rebuilt the operating model, tripled revenue through product and channel expansion, and executed a full exit in 2021.

Most recently, I served as Integrator (COO) at Jetstream Hospitality Solutions, a Vancouver-based hospitality SaaS company. I owned all operational functions — customer onboarding, account management, customer success, sales, and marketing — and built the systems and team structure to support sustainable growth.

Through Udow Ventures, I hold and manage this portfolio while actively seeking my next acquisition. I've been a founder, an acquirer, and an operator inside someone else's business. Right now I'm looking for the right established business to run — and I know what to do with one when I find it.
